Web Design

Web design involves the planning and creation of websites. It encompasses a wide range of skills, including graphic design, user experience (UX), and interface design. The goal is to create aesthetically pleasing, functional, and user-friendly websites that provide a positive user experience.

Web Development

Web development refers to the process of building and maintaining websites and web applications. It involves coding and programming, using languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and back-end technologies to bring websites to life. Web development focuses on the functionality, performance, and interactivity of a website.

Advetising & SEO

Advertising and Search Engine Optimization (SEO) are critical components of online marketing. Advertising involves promoting products or services through paid channels such as Google Ads, social media, and display networks. SEO, on the other hand, is the practice of optimizing a website's content and structure to rank higher on search engines organically.
